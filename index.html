PLACES TO VISIT IN QATAR

UM-ALSENEEM PARK
Um-AlSeneem Park boasts the longest air conditioned walking lane at a staggering 1km long and is a nice plce to visit toescape the bustle of the city
Lusail Downtown
Lusail Downtown is Qatar's newest city equipped with the latest and most advanced technology allowing it to be one of the best sustainable cities in the world . Come and look at the amazing sites and the World Cup final stadium and the events that always happens there
Doha Quest
Doha Quest is the home to anyone who loves adrinaline as it is the house to the longest indoor rollercoster and the hieghtest free fall ride in the world. Visit and test the limits of your adventerous spirit
